- **Conversation Threads**: Organized message threads with full context
- **Search & Filters**: Full-text search across all messages

- **Notifications**: Customizable alerts and notifications
- **Message Management**: Archive, star, and organize conversations
- **Rich Messages**: Support for text, images, and files
