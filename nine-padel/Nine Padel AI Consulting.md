### **HOW**

We model the business across **six core dimensions:**

1. **Group** – the business entity (Nine Padel Store)
    
2. **People** – owners, staff, and customers
    
3. **Things** – products, brands, orders, guides, reviews

Each element is mapped into a structured **Knowledge Graph** and **data dictionary** so that every AI system — chat, email, or analytics — can speak the same language.
